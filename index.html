Looking for a delicious and unique experience for your event?

Welcome to Milanga & Co, where Argentine culinary tradition meets modern flair! At Milanga & Co, we take pride in crafting unforgettable dining experiences inspired by the rich flavors of Argentina. Our journey began with a passion for sharing the authentic tastes of our homeland with the world.

Founded by a group of friends united by their love for Argentine cuisine, Milanga & Co has grown from humble beginnings into a renowned catering company known for its exceptional quality and dedication to excellence. Drawing from family recipes passed down through generations, we infuse every dish with the warmth and nostalgia of home-cooked meals shared with loved ones.

From the sizzle of our sizzling choripan to the melt-in-your-mouth goodness of our provoleta, each item on our menu is carefully crafted using the finest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. Whether you're craving the comforting embrace of locro or indulging in the sweetness of our alfajores, every bite tells a story of heritage and hospitality.

Driven by our commitment to customer satisfaction, we strive to exceed expectations with every event we cater. Whether it's an intimate gathering, corporate function, or grand celebration, our team works tirelessly to ensure that your special occasion is nothing short of extraordinary.

Join us at Milanga & Co and experience the magic of Argentine cuisine brought to life with passion and perfection. Let us take you on a culinary journey you won't soon forget.
