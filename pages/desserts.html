The Grand Finale

Save room for sweetness as you explore our enchanting array of desserts, each one a tempting finale to your dining experience. Indulge in the creamy decadence of our flan, a velvety custard topped with luscious caramel and a dollop of whipped cream. Or satisfy your sweet tooth with the classic simplicity of our panqueques con dulce de leche, delicate pancakes filled with rich caramelized milk. With every spoonful, you'll discover the irresistible allure of Argentine sweets.

Indulge in a delightful treat with alfajores, traditional Argentine cookies filled with dulce de leche and coated in powdered sugar or chocolate. Perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings!

Treat yourself to the creamy goodness of flan con dulce de leche y crema, a luscious Argentine dessert featuring silky caramel custard topped with rich dulce de leche and a dollop of whipped cream for the perfect finishing touch.

Indulge in a decadent dessert with panqueques con dulce de leche, delicate pancakes filled and drizzled with creamy caramelized milk, creating a sweet and satisfying treat.

Delight in the sweet flavors of pastelitos de membrillo and pastelitos de batata, traditional Argentine pastries filled with quince or sweet potato paste, fried to golden perfection, and dusted with powdered sugar for a delightful treat.
