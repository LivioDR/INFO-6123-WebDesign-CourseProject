The Main Attraction

Prepare to indulge in a symphony of flavors with our exquisite main courses, each dish crafted to perfection and served with passion. From the hearty comfort of our locro, to the irresistible allure of our milanesa napolitana, layered with tangy tomato sauce and melted cheese, every bite is a celebration of Argentine culinary tradition. So sit back, relax, and savor the essence of Argentina with every mouthwatering morsel.

Delight in the irresistible flavors of fugazzeta, a mouthwatering Argentine pizza topped with caramelized onions, gooey mozzarella cheese, and often seasoned with oregano and black pepper.

Savor the rich and hearty flavors of locro, a traditional Argentine stew made with corn, beans, meat (often beef or pork), and a variety of vegetables, simmered to perfection with spices for a comforting meal.

Treat your taste buds to matambre a la pizza, a delicious Argentine dish featuring thinly sliced beef flank steak topped with melted cheese, tomato sauce, and sometimes adorned with olives or peppers.

Indulge in a classic Argentine favorite with milanesa napolitana, featuring breaded and fried beef or chicken cutlets topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and often slices of ham, creating a mouthwatering combination of flavors and textures.
